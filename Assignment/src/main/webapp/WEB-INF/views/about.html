ABOUT US


ABC Group, a leading business organisation in Mauritius, is structured in five main clusters: Automobile, Banking, Financial & Insurance Services, Foods, Shipping & Logistics. Founded in 1931 by Sir Moilin Jean Ah Chuen, the company started its operations as a retail grocery store in Port Louis under the name of ABC, meaning “Au Bazar Central” (At the Central Marketplace) and “Aux Bonnes Choses”. It soon increased its business to include the wholesale segment and by the mid-forties had already well established itself in the importation & distribution of foodstuff & beverages.

Subsequently over the years it gradually diversified and expanded its activities to become a successful conglomerate comprising the five main clusters. The ABC Group now looks forward to further diversification on a regional scale with already a pioneering penetration in Hong Kong, Kenya and Seychelles.
